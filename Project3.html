Project 3: Downtown Turning Dark

June 2nd, 2025 — At this point I've only been downtown after dark once, the year before I started photography. Anyone, even people who don't have an interest in photography, must admit that night photography makes subjects look more interesting. But taking photos with very low light makes choosing the correct settings quite difficult.

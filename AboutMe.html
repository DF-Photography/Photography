About Me

Hello! I’m David Fernandez, a 20-year-old photographer from Chicago, Illinois. I focus on landscape, architecture, street, and non-person portrait photography. I picked up this hobby after taking Art-170A at NEIU, and it’s become something I really enjoy growing and experimenting with.

I don’t see photography as a career—I simply love exploring the city and capturing the moments, structures, and scenes that stand out to me. Each of my projects includes my ten best photos from the day, along with the camera settings, in case anyone wants to learn or follow along on their own photography journey.

If you’d like to see more of my work or connect with me, you can follow me on Instagram at @2005_davidf.

About Me

Hello, I’m David Fernandez, a 20-year-old hobbyist photographer from Chicago, Illinois. I specialize in landscape, architecture, street, and non-person portrait photography. I started this hobby when I signed up for Art-170A at Northeastern Illinois University and decided to continue to improve my skills in this hobby of mine.
